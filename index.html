● 96% High

● 91% High
We enforce RBAC with least-privilege principles. Privileged access requires MFA and is reviewed quarterly. All access is logged via AWS CloudTrail.
↳ Source: Access Control Policy v1.8 · Section 2.3
Do you conduct penetration testing?
● 68% Medium
Annual penetration testing is conducted by third-party firms. Results are reviewed by the security team and remediation tracked to closure.
