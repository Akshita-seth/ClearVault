// 03. CONFIDENCE SCORING ENGINE

Not just answers.
Trustworthy answers.

Every answer ClearVault generates comes with a confidence score based on retrieval similarity, source coverage, and answer completeness.

80–100%
High Confidence
Strong semantic match found in knowledge base. Multiple policy sections confirm the answer. Source citations available.
→ AUTO-APPROVED · READY TO SEND
50–79%
Medium Confidence
Partial match found. Answer is plausible but based on limited or indirect policy references. Recommended for review.
→ REVIEW RECOMMENDED
0–49%
Low Confidence
No strong match in knowledge base. Question may be outside documented scope. Human expertise required.
→ FLAGGED FOR HUMAN REVIEW
